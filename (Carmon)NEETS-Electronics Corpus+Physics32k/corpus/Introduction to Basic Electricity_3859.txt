A­drawback­of­direct-coupled­amplifiers­is­that­
they­are­not­stable.­Any­change­in­the­output­current­
of­the­first­stage­is­amplified­by­the­second­stage.­This­
occurs­because­the­second­stage­is­essentially­biased­by­
the­first­stage.­To­improve­the­stability­requires­the­use­
of­expensive­precision­components.
