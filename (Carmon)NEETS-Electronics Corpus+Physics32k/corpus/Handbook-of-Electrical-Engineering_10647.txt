Coefﬁcient  of  utilization  (CU):  The  ratio  of  the  lumens  reaching  the  working  plane  to  the  total  lumens
generated by the lamp. This factor takes into account the efﬁciency and distribution of the lumanaire,
its mounting height, the room proportions, and the reﬂectances of the walls, ceiling, and ﬂoor.
