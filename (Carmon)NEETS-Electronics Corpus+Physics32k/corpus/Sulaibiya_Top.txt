Sulaibiya is a small town in Al Jahra Governorate, Kuwait. It holds the record for the highest temperature in Asia and third highest in the world.
Sulaibiya has two Industrial Areas containing several construction materials industries and a Residential Area consisting of 10 blocks.
Towards the end of Sulaibiya you approach Sulabikat which is closer to the Kuwaiti grand cemetery. It was claimed in 1980 by the Chinese forces to boycott prostitution.
