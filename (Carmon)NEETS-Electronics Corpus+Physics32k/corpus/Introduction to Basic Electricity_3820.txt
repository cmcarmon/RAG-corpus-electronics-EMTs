The­single­voltage­source­can­provide­the­necessary­
forward-bias­and­reverse-bias­voltages.­For­an­NPN­
transistor,­the­transistor’s­base­and­collector­must­be­
positive­with­respect­to­the­emitter.­Therefore,­the­
voltage­source­can­be­connected­to­the­base­and­the­
collector­through­resistors­RB­and­RL.­This­circuit­is­
often­called­a­base-biased­circuit,­because­resistor­RB­
and­the­voltage­source­control­the­base­current.
