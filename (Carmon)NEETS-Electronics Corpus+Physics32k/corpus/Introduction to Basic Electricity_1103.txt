Power tools are classified as commercial or 
consumer,  with commercial power tools used in 
business and industry, and consumer power tools 
used in a household. Power tools are useful in the 
following tasks: drilling, cutting, fastening, grind-
ing, heating, painting, polishing, routing, sanding, 
shaping, and more. Power tools are also classified 
as either portable or stationary. Portable power 
tools are handheld and offer mobility. Stationary 
power tools cannot be moved but offer accuracy 
and speed. Stationary power tools are available for 
both metalworking and woodworking. Drill presses 
and band saws are used for both woodworking and 
metalworking.
