Numerous excellent reviews on the subject of magnetooptics have been published over the years, for example,
J.F. Dillon, Jr., “Magnetooptics and its uses,” Journal of Magnetism and Magnetic Materials, vol. 31–34, pp. 1–9,
1983; M.J. Freiser, “A survey of magnetooptic effects,” IEEE Transactions on Magnetics, vol. MAG-4, pp. 152–161,
1968;  G. A.  Smolenskii,  R.V.  Pisarev,  and  I.G.  Sinii, “Birefringence  of  light  in  magnetically  ordered  crystals,”
Sov.  Phys.  Usp.,  vol.  18,  pp.  410–429,  1976;  and A.M.  Prokhorov,  G.A.  Smolenskii,  and A.N.  Ageev, “Optical
phenomena  in  thin-ﬁlm  magnetic  waveguides  and  their  technical  application,”  Sov.  Phys.  Usp.,  vol.  27,  pp.
339–362, 1984.
