Military Standards. The standards and requirements related to the electromagnetic interference and suscep-
tibility for the Military in the United States are described in the MIL-STD-461C document. Electromagnetic
interference is deﬁned as the radiated and conducted energy emitted from the device. Electromagnetic suscep-
tibility is deﬁned as the amount of radiated or conducted energy that the device can withstand without degrading
its performance.
