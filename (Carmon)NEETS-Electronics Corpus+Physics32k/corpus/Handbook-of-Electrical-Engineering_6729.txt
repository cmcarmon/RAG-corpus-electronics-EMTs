There are many conferences and workshops that provide up-to-date coverage in the computer communications
area.  Among  these  are  the  IEEE  INFOCOM  and  ACM  SIGCOMM  conferences  and  the  IEEE  Computer
Communications Workshop, which specialize in computer communications and are held annually. In addition,
IEEE GLOBCOM (annual), IEEE ICC (annual), IFIPS ICCC (biannual), and the International Telecommuni-
cations Congress (biannual) regularly feature a substantial number of paper and panel sessions in networking.
