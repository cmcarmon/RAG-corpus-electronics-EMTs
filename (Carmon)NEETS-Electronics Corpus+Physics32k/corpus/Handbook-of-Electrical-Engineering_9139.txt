• ISO 7816-11 — Identiﬁcation cards — Integrated circuit(s) cards with contacts — Part 11: Card structure
and enhanced functions for multi-application use. Deﬁnes an enhanced logical structure for multi-appli-
cation  cards  to  support  security  with  applications  loaded  after  issuance,  prerequisites  for  loading  of  an
application after issuance, a mechanism to support an application life cycle independent of the card life cycle,
interapplication security issues, including storage partitioning.
