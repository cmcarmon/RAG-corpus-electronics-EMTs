TDM): The time slots are not preassigned for a signal or
connection path. They are allocated on a demand basis.
There is no waste of bandwidth. A terminal number is
included in the message to identify where the message
came from. The output rate is designed to be less than the
sum of data rates of all inputs. At the peak load, total
inputs might exceed output capacity, then backlog will be
built. The trade-off is between the size of buffers and the
rates of total inputs to be supported.
