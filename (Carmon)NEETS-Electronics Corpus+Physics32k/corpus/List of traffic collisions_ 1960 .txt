July 24 – A charter bus collides with a regular route bus, the charter bus plunging 270 feet into a valley along the toll road of Mount Hiei, Ōtsu, Shiga, Japan, killing 30 people and injuring another 18.
September 14 – A truck struck a suburban bus which subsequently crashed on an olive tree between Athens and Korinth. The collision claimed 11 lives (10 passengers and the driver).
