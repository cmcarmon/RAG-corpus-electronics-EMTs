 Inductance exhibits the same effect on current 
in an electric circuit as inertia does on velocity of 
a mechanical object. It takes more work to start a 
load moving than it does to keep it moving because 
the load possesses the property of inertia. Inertia is 
the characteristic of mass that opposes a change in 
 velocity. Once current is moving through a conduc-
tor, inductance helps to keep it moving. The effects of 
inductance are sometimes desirable and other times 
undesirable.
