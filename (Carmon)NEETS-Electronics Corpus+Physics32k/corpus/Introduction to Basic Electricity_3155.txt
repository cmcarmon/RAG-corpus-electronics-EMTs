After completing this chapter, the student will be 
able to:
Describe how a transistor is constructed and its 
two different configurations.
Draw and label the schematic symbol for an 
NPN transistor and a PNP transistor.
Identify the ways of classifying transistors.
Identify the function of a transistor using a 
reference manual and the identification number 
(2NXXXX).
Identify commonly used transistor packages.
Describe how to bias a transistor for operation.
Explain how to test a transistor with both a 
transistor tester and an ohmmeter.
Describe the process used for substituting a 
transistor.
