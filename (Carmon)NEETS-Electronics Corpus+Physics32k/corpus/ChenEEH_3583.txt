Digital ﬁlters are present in various digital signal processing
applications related to speech, audio, image, video, and multi-
rate processing systems as well as in communication systems,
CD players, digital radio, television, and control systems.
Digital ﬁlters can have either ﬁxed (Antoniou, 1993; Jackson,
1996; Oppenheim and Schafer, 1989; Mitra, 2001; Diniz, 2002)
or adaptive coefﬁcients (Diniz, 2002).
