The 1963 Ankara mid-air collision occurred on Friday, 1 February, 1963 over Ankara, Turkey when Middle East Airlines Flight 265, a Vickers 754D Viscount completing the flight from Cyprus, came in for landing and collided in the air with a Turkish Air Force Douglas C-47A; after which both planes fell directly to the city below them. In total, 104 people died in the accident, including 87 on the ground.
