After completing this chapter, the student 
will be able to:
Describe an oscillator and its purpose.
Identify the main requirements of an oscillator.
Explain how a tank circuit operates and 
describe its relationship to an oscillator.
Draw a block diagram of an oscillator.
Identify LC, crystal, and RC sinusoidal oscillator 
circuits.
Identify nonsinusoidal relaxation oscillator 
circuits.
Draw examples of sinusoidal and nonsinusoidal 
oscillators.
