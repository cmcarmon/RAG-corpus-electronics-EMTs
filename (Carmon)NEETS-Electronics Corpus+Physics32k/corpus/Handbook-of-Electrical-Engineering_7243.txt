Hidden Costs
Signiﬁcant problems arise when these moves or changes are implemented. There is always the disruption of
the workers involved in the move or change, not to mention the loss in productivity. The problems, however,
become  much  more  involved  when  dealing  with  whole  departments  and  more  complex  user  equipment.  In
fact, surveyed ﬁrms responded that when a relocation takes place, over 60% of the time it involves the movement
of an entire department.
