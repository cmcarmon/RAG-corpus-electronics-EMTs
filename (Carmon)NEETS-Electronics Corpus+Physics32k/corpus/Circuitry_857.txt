Problems sometimes arise when a standard drill or 
hand drill is unable to adequately grip a miniature 
twist drill bit. In such cases one should make use of 
a miniature  pin  chuck  or  a  drill  fitted  with  an 
enlarged shank (usually of 2.4 mm diameter).
