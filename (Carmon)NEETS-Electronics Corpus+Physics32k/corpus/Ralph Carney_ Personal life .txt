Carney's nephew Patrick Carney is the drummer for the Black Keys. The two collaborated on the theme music to the Netflix original series BoJack Horseman.
Carney died on December 17, 2017, at the age of 61, from head injuries sustained falling down steps in his home in Portland, Oregon the previous day.  He was survived by his ex-wife Deena Zacharin and their daughter, Hedda, as well as his soulmate Megan Hinchliffe.
