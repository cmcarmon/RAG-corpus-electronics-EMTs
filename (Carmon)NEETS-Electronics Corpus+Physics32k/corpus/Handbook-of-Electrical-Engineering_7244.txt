The toll of wait time and down time on productivity varies greatly and is difﬁcult to quantify, but certainly
is signiﬁcant and costly. In today’s increasingly mobile working environment, it is likely to grow. The situation
is  exacerbated  by  relocations  and  additions  which  require  reconstructions,  thereby  continuing  to  add  to  the
effective cost of a move, add, or change.
