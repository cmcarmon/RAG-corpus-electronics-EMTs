Rise time, trise 
This  is  the  time  measured  between  the  10%  and 
90%  points  on  the  rising  (or  positive-going) edge 
of the pulse. 
 
Fall time, tfall 
This  is  the  time  measured  between  the  90%  and 
10% points on the falling (or negative-going) edge 
of the pulse.
