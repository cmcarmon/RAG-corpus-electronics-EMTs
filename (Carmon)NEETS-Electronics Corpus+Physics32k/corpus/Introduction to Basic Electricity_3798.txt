The­input­signal­controls­current­flow­in­the­tran-
sistor.­This­in­turn­controls­the­voltage­through­the­
load.­The­transistor­circuit­is­designed­to­take­voltage­
from­an­external­power­source­(VCC)­and­apply­it­to­a­
load­resistor­(RL)­in­the­form­of­an­output­voltage.­The­
output­voltage­is­controlled­by­a­small­input­voltage.
