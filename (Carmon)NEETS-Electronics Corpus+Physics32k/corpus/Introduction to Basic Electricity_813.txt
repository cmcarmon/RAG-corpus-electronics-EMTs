Presentation	 programs,  such  as  Microsoft’s 
 PowerPoint® or Apple’s Keynote®, are used to display 
information, normally in a slide format; the presenta-
tion is referred to as a slide show and is often paired 
with an oral presentation. A presentation program 
typically includes three major functions:
■● An editor to allow text to be inserted and formatted
■● A process for inserting and altering the graphic
