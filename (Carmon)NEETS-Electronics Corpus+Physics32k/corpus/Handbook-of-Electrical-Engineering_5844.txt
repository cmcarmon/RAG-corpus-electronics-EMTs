The connected load or demand can be estimated accurately in residential and industrial areas. The connected
load  or  demand  is  the  sum  of  continuous  ratings  of  apparatus  connected  to  the  system.  However,  not  all
equipment is used simultaneously. The actual load in a system is signiﬁcantly lower than the connected load.
