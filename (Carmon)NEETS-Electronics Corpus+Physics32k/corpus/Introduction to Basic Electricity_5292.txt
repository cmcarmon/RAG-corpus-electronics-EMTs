Component	placement	errors include the fol-
lowing: wrong component or component not in the 
correct location, components mounted in backwards, 
or multi-lead components with leads inserted improp-
erly. Wiring	errors include the following: a wire in-
stalled in the wrong location, a wire missing, a wire 
that is broken (open), and a wire that is installed with 
a faulty solder connection.
