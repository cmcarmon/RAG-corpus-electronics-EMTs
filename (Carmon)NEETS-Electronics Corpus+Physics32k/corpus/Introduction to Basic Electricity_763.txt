Multisim  includes  a  number  of  measurement 
 instruments that can be used to interactively measure 
the behavior of circuits. It provides several  virtual	
	instruments  for  taking  circuit  measurements, 
 including these:
■● Analog and digital multimeters
■● Bode plotter
■● Logic analyzer
■● Oscilloscope
■● Signal generator
■● Wattmeter
