The primary signaling rate of a carrier. A 9600 “baud” modem transfers data at 2400 baud, but
the  signal  rate  is  4  bits  per  cycle  allowing  for  a  transfer  rate  of  9600 bps.  Baud  and  bps  are  used
synonymously but they not the same.
Bulletin board system. An electronic multi-user system that often includes a message database people
