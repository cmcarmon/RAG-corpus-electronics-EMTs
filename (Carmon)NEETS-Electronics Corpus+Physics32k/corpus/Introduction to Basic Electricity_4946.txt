After completing this chapter, the student will be 
able to:
Describe the fundamental process for making a 
printed circuit board.
Design and lay out a printed circuit board from 
a schematic diagram.
Discuss how to transfer a design to a copper-
clad board using the hand transfer, direct 
transfer, or screenprinting technique.
Identify techniques to remove the excess copper 
from a copper-clad board on which a design is 
formed.
Explain how to drill the appropriate holes in an 
etched printed circuit board.
