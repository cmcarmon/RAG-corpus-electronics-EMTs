The Digital Mirror Device (DMD) developed and sold by
Texas instruments has also been on the market for several years.
It is a display device used in projectors for conference rooms and
movie theaters. It will be noted in the subsection on actuators.
The DMD contains more than one-half million micromirrors in
one version and more than one million mirrors in another.
