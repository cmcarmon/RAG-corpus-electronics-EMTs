In what follows, we provide a brief description of digital
image processing techniques. Section 4.1 deals with image
sampling, and Section 4.2 describes image quantization. In
Section 4.3, some image enhancement techniques are given.
Section 4.4 analyzes image restoration. Image compression, or
coding, is presented in Section 4.5. Finally, Section 4.6 intro-
duces the main issues involved in image analysis.
