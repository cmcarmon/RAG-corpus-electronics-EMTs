Audiobook version include:
A Martian Time-Slip audiobook — read by Tom Parker, unabridged, approximately 9 hours over 6 audio cassettess — was released in 1998.
A second unabridged audiobook version of Martian Time-Slip was released in 2007. Read by Grover Gardner, it runs approximately 9.5 hours over 8 CDs. It was released under the title Martian Time-Slip and The Golden Man, and also includes a reading of Dick's short story "The Golden Man."
