The journal IEEE Transactions on Medical Imaging describes advances in imaging techniques and image pro-
cessing.  Investigative  Radiology,  published  by  the Association  of  University  Radiologists,  emphasizes  research
carried out by hospital-based physicists and engineers. Radiology, published by the North American Society of
Radiologists, contains articles which emphasize clinical applications of imaging technology. Diagnostic Imaging,
publishing  by  Miller  Freeman,  Inc.,  is  a  good  source  of  review  articles  and  information  on  the  imaging
marketplace.
