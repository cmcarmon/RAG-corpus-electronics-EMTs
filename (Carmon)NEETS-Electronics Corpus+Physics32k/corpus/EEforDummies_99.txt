Solar panels are becoming increasingly 
popular for supplying electrical power 
to homes and businesses. If you scour 
the Internet, you’ll find lots of informa-
tion on how you can make your own 
solar panels — for just a couple of hun-
dred dollars and a willingness to try. 
You can read more about this topic in 
Solar Power Your Home For Dummies, 
2nd Edition, by Rik DeGunther (Wiley 
Publishing, Inc.).
