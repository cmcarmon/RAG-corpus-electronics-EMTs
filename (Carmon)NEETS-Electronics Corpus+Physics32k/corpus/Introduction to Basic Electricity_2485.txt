After	completing	this	chapter,	the	student	will	be	
able	to:
Identify	the	types	of	meters	available	for		
AC	measurements.
Identify	the	types	of	meter	movements	used	to	
make	AC	measurements.
Explain	the	function	of	an	oscilloscope.
Identify	the	basic	parts	of	an	oscilloscope	and	
explain	their	functions.
Demonstrate	the	proper	setup	of	an	
oscilloscope.
Describe	how	to	use	an	oscilloscope	to	take	a	
measurement.
Explain	how	a	counter	works.
Identify	the	basic	parts	of	a	counter.
