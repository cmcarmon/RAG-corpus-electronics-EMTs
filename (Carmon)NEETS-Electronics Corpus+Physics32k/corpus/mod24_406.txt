As stated before, fiber alignment depends on an accurate hole through the center of the ferrule.

ferrule. Drilling an accurate hole through the entire metal ferrule can be difficult. To improve fiber
alignment, some metal ferrule connectors use precision watch-jeweled centering. In precision watch-
