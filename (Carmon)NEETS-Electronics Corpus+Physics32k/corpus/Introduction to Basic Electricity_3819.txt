Resistor­RB,­connected­between­the­base­and­the­
voltage­source,­controls­the­amount­of­current­­flowing­
out­of­the­base.­The­base­current­flowing­through­
­resistor­RB­creates­a­voltage­across­it.­Most­of­the­voltage­
from­the­source­is­dropped­across­it.­A­small­amount­
of­voltage­drops­across­the­transistor’s­­base-to-emitter­
junction,­providing­the­proper­forward­bias.
