Clearly Deﬁned Service Expectations and Trafﬁc
Descriptions
To enhance the current Internet to support service guarantees,
it is necessary to deﬁne such service guarantees in clear math-
ematical terms. QoS quantiﬁes the level of service that a
distributed multimedia application expects from the commu-
nication network. In general, three QoS parameters are of
prime interest: bandwidth, delay, and reliability.
