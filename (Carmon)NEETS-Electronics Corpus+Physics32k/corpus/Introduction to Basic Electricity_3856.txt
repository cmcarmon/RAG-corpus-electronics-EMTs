In­ a­ transformer-coupled­ circuit,­ the­ two­
­amplifier­stages­are­coupled­through­the­transformer­
­(Figure 37-19).­The­transformer­can­effectively­match­
a­high-impedance­source­to­a­low-impedance­load.­
The­disadvantage­is­that­transformers­are­large­and­
­expensive.­Also,­like­the­inductor-coupled­amplifier,­
the­transformer-coupled­amplifier­is­useful­only­for­a­
narrow­band­of­frequencies.
