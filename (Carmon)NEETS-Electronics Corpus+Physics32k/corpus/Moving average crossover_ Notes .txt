Golden cross-There are several types of moving average cross traders use in trading. When 200 days simple moving average cross 50 days simple moving average it is called a golden cross. The golden cross is widely accepted.
Silver cross-When 50 Exponential moving average cross above or below 100 exponential moving average it is known as a silver cross. Silver cross invented by a Successful forex trader S.A.Hasib. S.A.Hasib believe that exponential moving average helps to understand current market conditions. The exponential moving average also acts as a dynamic support and resistance.
