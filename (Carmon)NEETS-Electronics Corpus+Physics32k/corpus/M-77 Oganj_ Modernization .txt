Military Technical Institute Belgrade on demand from Serbia army has produced modernization program for Oganj M-77. Fixed barrels will be replaced with container system similar like LRSVM Morava and it will include modern navigation and fire control system. Containers will allow Oganj to use many different rockets including 107mm, Plamen 128mm, Grad 122mm and Oganj 128mm.
