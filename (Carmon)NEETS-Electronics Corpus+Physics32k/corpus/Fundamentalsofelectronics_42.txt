Solutions to Exercises in Chapter 1
Solution to Exercise 1.1 (p. 6)
As cos (α + β) = cos (α) cos (β) − sin (α) sin (β), cos (2π (f + 1) n) = cos (2πf n) cos (2πn) − sin (2πf n) sin (2πn) =
cos (2πf n).
