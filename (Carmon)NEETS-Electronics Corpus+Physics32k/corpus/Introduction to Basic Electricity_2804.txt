Resonance­is­a­phenomenon­that­occurs­in­many­fields,­
including­electronics.­A­resonance­device­­produces­
a­broadening­and­dampening­effect.­In­­electronics,­
­resonant­circuits­pass­desired­frequencies­and­reject­all­
others.­The­ability­of­a­series­or­parallel­combination­
of­XL­or­XC­to­produce­resonance­provides­a­number­of­
unique­applications.­Resonant­circuits­make­it­­possible­
for­a­radio­or­television­­receiver­to­tune­in­and­receive­
a­station­at­a­particular­frequency.­The­tuning­circuit­
consists­of­a­coil­of­wire­in­parallel­with­a­capacitor.­
Parallel­tuned­circuits­have­maximum­impedance­at­­
resonant­frequencies.­Tuned­circuits­are­vital­to­a­
­variety­of­types­of­communication­equipment,­from­
radios­to­radar.
