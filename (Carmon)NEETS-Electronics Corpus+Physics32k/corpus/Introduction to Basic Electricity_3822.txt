The­circuit­shown­in­Figure­37-7­is­unstable,­be-
cause­it­cannot­compensate­for­changes­in­the­bias­
current­with­no­signal­applied.­Temperature­changes­
cause­the­transistor’s­internal­resistance­to­vary,­
which­causes­the­bias­currents­to­change.­This­shifts­
the­transistor­operating­point,­reducing­the­gain­of­
the­transistor.­This­process­is­referred­to­as­thermal	
instability.
