January UK – A bus skids into a bus queue at Bargeddie, near Glasgow, Scotland, killing 8 people and injuring 12.
November 24 Sweden – A trolleybus on line 96 collided with a truck loaded with concrete slabs that were heading in the opposite direction. The bus drove off Essingebron in Stockholm and 11 people died.
December 19 Argentina – A commuter train hits a truck carrying hikers in the Greater Buenos Aires, killing 44 and injuring 25 others.
