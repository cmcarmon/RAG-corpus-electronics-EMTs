Developing good work habits is a matter of devel-
oping proper attitudes and values toward work. To be 
successful at work:
■● Develop a mission, goals, and a strong work ethic.
■● Have good attendance and be punctual.
■● Value time by staying on task.
■● Operate with neatness, orderliness, and speed.
■● Work smarter, not harder.
■● Appreciate the importance of rest and  relaxation
