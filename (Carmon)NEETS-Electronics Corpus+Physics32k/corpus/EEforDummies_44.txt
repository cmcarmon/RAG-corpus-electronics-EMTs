The term electricity is ambiguous, often contra-
dictory, and can lead to confusion, even among 
scientists  and  teachers.  Generally  speaking, 
electricity  has  to  do  with  how  certain  types 
of particles in nature interact with each other 
when in close proximity.

explore the field of electronics, you’re better 
off using other, more precise, terminology to 
describe all things electric. Here are some of
