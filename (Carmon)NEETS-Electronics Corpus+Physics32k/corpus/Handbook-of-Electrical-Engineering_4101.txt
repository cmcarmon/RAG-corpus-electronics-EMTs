*Except  as  provided  in  paragraph  (g),  fundamental  emissions
from intentional radiators operating under this Section shall not be
located  in  the  frequency  bands  54–72  MHz,  76–88  MHz,  174–216
MHz, or 470–806 MHz. However, operation within these frequency
bands is permitted under other sections of this part, e.g., §§ 15.231
and 15.241.
