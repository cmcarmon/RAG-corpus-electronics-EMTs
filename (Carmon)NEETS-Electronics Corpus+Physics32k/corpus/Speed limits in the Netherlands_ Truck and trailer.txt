In the Netherlands, heavy trucks and vehicles and trailer combinations with a total weight over 3.5t (7 700 lb) and busses have an overall speed limit of 80 km/h (50 mph) on expressways and motorways. On roads or lanes with a (general or posted) speed limit under 80km/h, these vehicles must abide by this lower limit. Trucks and vehicles above 3.5t are required to have a speed limiting device to prevent them from speeding above 90 km/h.
Cars and trucks pulling a trailer with a total (car+trailer) weight under 3.5t have an overall speed limit of 90 km/h (56 mph), and coaches equipped with seat belts, categorized as T100, have an overall speed limit of 100 km/h (62 mph). This following the same regulation as standing above. Light commercial vehicles under 3.5t don't have special speed limits.
