After	completing	this	chapter,	the	student	will	be	
able	to:
Identify	who	is	responsible	for	certification	of	
electronics	technicians.
Discuss	why	getting	a	certification	is	important	
for	becoming	an	electronics	technician.
Discuss	the	different	levels	and	certifications	
available	for	electronics	technicians.
Identify	what	knowledge	is	required	for	each	
level	of	the	certification	exams.
Identify	who	is	eligible	to	become	certified	as	
an	electronics	technician.
Describe	how	to	prepare	for	a	career	in	
electronics.
Discuss	the	educational	requirements	for	
becoming	an	electronics	technician.
