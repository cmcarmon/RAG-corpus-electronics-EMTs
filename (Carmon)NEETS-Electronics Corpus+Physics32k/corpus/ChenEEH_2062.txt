The following TPrTN models the Five Dining Philosophers
problem, shown in Figure 9.1, in which ﬁve philosophers are
denoted by ﬁve integer tokens 0 to 4 and ﬁve chopsticks are also
denoted by ﬁve integer tokens 0 to 4. Places p1 and p3 stand for the
Thinking and Eating states of philosophers, respectively. Place p2
deﬁnes the availability of chopsticks. Transitions t1 and t2 stand
for the actions of Pick up and Put down chopsticks, respectively.
