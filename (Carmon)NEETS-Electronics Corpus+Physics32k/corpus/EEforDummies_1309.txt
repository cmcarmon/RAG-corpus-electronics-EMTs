battery: A power source that uses an electrochemical reaction to produce a 
positive voltage at one terminal and a negative voltage at the other terminal. 
This process involves placing two different types of metal in a certain type of 
chemical. See also alkaline battery, lithium battery, nickel‐cadmium (NiMH) 
battery, nickel‐metal hydride (NiCd) battery and, zinc‐carbon battery.
