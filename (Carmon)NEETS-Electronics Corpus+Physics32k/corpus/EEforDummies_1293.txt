Allied Electronics is what’s known as a stocking distributor. It offers goods 
from a variety of manufacturers, and most parts are available for immedi-
ate shipping from Allied’s warehouse in Fort Worth, Texas. Allied is geared 
toward the electronics professional, but it welcomes hobbyists, too. The 
Allied catalog is huge, and it’s available on the website as well as in print. You 
can also find parts using the search feature on Allied’s website.
