E.I.A.  prints  a  wide  range  of  literature  on  electronics  components,  computers  and  industrial  electronics,
communications  and  services.  E.I.A.  also  administers  many  committees  for  standardization,  safety,  etc.,  for
cathode  ray  tubes  and  other  components.  E.I.A.  offers  a  complete  Electronics  Technology  Curriculum  and
technical training books, tapes, etc.
