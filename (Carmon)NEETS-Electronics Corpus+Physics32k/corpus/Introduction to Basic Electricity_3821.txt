The­input­signal­is­applied­between­the­transistor’s­
base­and­emitter­or­between­the­input­terminal­and­
ground.­The­input­signal­either­aids­or­opposes­the­
forward­bias­across­the­emitter­junction.­This­causes­
the­collector­current­to­vary,­which­then­causes­the­
voltage­across­RL­to­vary.­The­output­signal­is­devel-
oped­between­the­output­terminal­and­ground.
