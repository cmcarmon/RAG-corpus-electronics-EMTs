After	completing	this	chapter,	the	student	will	be	
able	to:
Define	power	as	it	relates	to	electric	circuits.
State	the	relationship	of	current	and	voltage.
Solve	for	power	consumption	in	an	electrical	
circuit.
Determine	the	total	power	consumption	in	a	
series,	parallel,	or	series-parallel	circuit.
