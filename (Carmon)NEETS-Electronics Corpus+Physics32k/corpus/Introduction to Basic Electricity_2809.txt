Above­and­below­the­resonant­frequency­of­any­LC­
circuit,­the­circuit­behaves­as­any­standard­AC­circuit.­
Resonance­is­desired­with­radio­frequencies­in­tuning­
receivers­and­transmitters,­certain­industrial­equip-
ment,­and­test­equipment.­It­is­undesired­in­audio­
­amplifiers­and­power­supplies.­Resonant­circuits­are­
not­used­in­the­audio­bands­of­frequencies.
