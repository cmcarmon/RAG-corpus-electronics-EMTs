February 1 Haiti – A truck carrying many hitchhikers was hit by ten-wheel flatbed truck carrying coffee. The truck overturned and plunged into the Grande-Anse River, near Jérémie, Grand'Anse, Haiti, killing 70 people.
February 15 – A bus plunged into a 170-foot ravine near Pallasca Province, Ancash Region, Peru, killing 14 people.
April 10 – An overcrowded regular route bus blew a front tire and plunged into an irrigation canal near Mueang Chainat, Chainat Province, Thailand, killing 42 people and injuring 11.
April 20 Thailand – A local bus collided with a paramilitary troop tank at Nakhon Ratchasima, Thailand. The resulting explosion killed 25 people and injured nine.
14 May United States – 27 people die in the Carrollton bus disaster near Carrollton, Kentucky, after a collision with a drunk driver driving the wrong way on Interstate 71. It remains the deadliest drunk driving crash in American history.
July 18 Brazil – A Londrina-Campinas regular route bus swerved to avoid three cows crossing national road 369, then struck the rear of another bus near Cornelio Procopio, Parana, Brazil, killing 39 people.
12 August Iran – Two buses collide on the highway between Tehran and Tabriz in northern Iran, killing 30 people and injuring 50.
15 August Norway – 16 people, including 12 children, die in the Måbødalen bus crash when a bus rams into a concrete wall in a tunnel 60 miles east of Bergen, Norway.
August 23 Austria – A Hungarian tour bus lost control and slammed into a truck at Schwadorf near Vienna, Austria, killing 14 people and injuring 11.
22 September India – A bus falls into a ravine near Simla, India. 35 people die.
September 25 India – A bus plunged into Bhakra canal, smashing through the safety railing of a bridge near Jogwala, Sangrur district, Punjab, India, killing 67 people.
October 6 – A Kafr el Sheikh-Baltim regular-route bus was hit by a flatbed trailer on the outskirts of El-Mahalla El-Kubra, Egypt, killing 28 people and injuring 21.
October 9 – A Guwahati-Nalbari regular-route bus skidded off a road and crashed into a flooded pond on the outskirts of Guwahati, Assam, India, killing 50 people.
October 12 – A bus ignites after toppling into a ditch in Shaanxi, China, killing 43 people and injuring 40.
October 21 – Three separate buses collided with a refrigerator truck and overturned at Martorell, Catalonia, Spain, killing 14 people and injuring 65.
