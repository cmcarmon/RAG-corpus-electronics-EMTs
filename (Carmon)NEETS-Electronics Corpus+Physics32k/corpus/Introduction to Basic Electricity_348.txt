The associate-Level	Cet	exam is available to 
students in electronics or technician programs. This 
exam focuses only on basic electronics and is part of 
the full credit CET exam. The exam is multiple choice 
and covers basic electronics, math, DC and AC circuits, 
transistors, and troubleshooting. It must be passed with 
a score of 75% or better. A successful associate CET 
receives a wall certificate valid for four years and is 
eligible to join ISCET, the International Society of 
Certified Electronics Technicians. The Associate CET 
certification expires four years after being issued.
