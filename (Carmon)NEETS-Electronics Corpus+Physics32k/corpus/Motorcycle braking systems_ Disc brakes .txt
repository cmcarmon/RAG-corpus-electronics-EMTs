ABS BMW main article

The front suspension on the Yamaha GTS1000 released in 1993 was a single sided swingarm that amongst other characteristics aimed to reduce diving under braking. The design only allowed for a single front disc brake so a comparatively large 330mm disc was mated to a six-piston caliper, a world first on a production bike.
