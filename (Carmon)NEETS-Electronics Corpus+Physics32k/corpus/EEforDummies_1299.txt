RadioShack is perhaps the world’s most recognized source for hobby 
electronics, but alas, “The Shack” filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy in early 
2015 and the future of its brand is uncertain. As of mid‐2015, roughly half of 
RadioShack’s 4,000 retail stores were in the process of closing, but it’s likely 
you can still find a location within reasonable driving distance of your home. 
If you need a resistor, capacitor, or transistor right away, you definitely won’t 
find it at Wal‐Mart or BestBuy, but you may find it at your local RadioShack.
