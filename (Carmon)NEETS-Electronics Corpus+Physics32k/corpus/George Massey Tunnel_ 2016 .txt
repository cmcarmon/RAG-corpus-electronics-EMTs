March – Phase 3 consultation results are released, showing strong support for the replacement of the Massey Tunnel with a bridge
October – The BC Government requests 3 pre-qualified teams to participate in submitting Request for Proposals (RFP)
December – The BC Government announces that the Massey Tunnel replacement won't suffer from ice bomb issues (snow and ice releasing from overhead cables in warming conditions and falling onto vehicle deck) like the Port Mann and Alex Fraser bridges
