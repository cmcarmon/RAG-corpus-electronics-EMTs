Difference  (or  differential)  ampliﬁers  are  used  in  various  applications
where there is a need to amplify the difference between two input sig-
nals. They  are  ﬁrst  cousins  of  the  instrumentation  ampliﬁer,  the  most
useful and popular ampliﬁer, which we will discuss in Section 5.10.
