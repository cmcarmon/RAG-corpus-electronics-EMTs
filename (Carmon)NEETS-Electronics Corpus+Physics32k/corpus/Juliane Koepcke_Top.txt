Juliane Koepcke (b. 10 October 1954 in Lima, Peru), also known by her married name Juliane Diller, is a German mammalogist. As a teenager, Koepcke was the sole passenger to survive the LANSA Flight 508 plane crash and her story of surviving not only the crash but 11 days alone in the Amazon rainforest has received a steady amount of media attention over the past four decades.
