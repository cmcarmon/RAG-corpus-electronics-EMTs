After completing this chapter, the student will be 
able to:
Describe the functions of encoders, decoders, 
multiplexers, adders, subtractors, and 
comparators.
Identify the schematic symbols for encoders, 
decoders, multiplexers, adders, subtractors, and 
comparators.
Identify applications for combinational logic 
circuits.
Develop truth tables for the different 
combinational logic circuits.
