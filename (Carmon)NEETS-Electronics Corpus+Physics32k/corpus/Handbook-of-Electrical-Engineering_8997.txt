The  Society  for  Information  Display  (SID)  sponsors  a  yearly  symposium  at  which  a  large  amount  of
information on new developments in information display as well as tutorials and seminars on basic information
display  topics  are  presented  and  made  available  in  published  form.  In  addition,  it  publishes  two  journals,
namely, Proceedings of the Society for Information Display and Information Display. Other relevant meetings and
publications  are  those  sponsored  by  the  Computer  Society  and  Electron  Devices  groups  of  the  IEEE,  the
SIGGRAPH group of the Association for Computing Machines (ACM), and the National Computer Graphics
Association (NCGA).
