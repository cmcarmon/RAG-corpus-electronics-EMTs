	● Calculate using exponents, reciprocals, powers, 
and roots. A base number and an exponent rep-
resent a power. The base number is the number 
to be multiplied. The exponent, a small number 
written above and to the right of the base number, 
tells how many times the base number is being 
multiplied. Exponents are shorthand for repeated 
multiplication of a number by itself.
