Under  the  bill,  lighting  manufacturers  will  have  3  years  to  stop  making  F96T12  and  F96T12/HO  8  ft
ﬂuorescent lamps and some types of incandescent reﬂectors. Standard F40 lamps except in the SP and SPX or
equivalent types of high color rendering lamps would also fade away. General service incandescent lamps to
be axed would include those from 30 to 100 W, in 115 to 130 V ratings, having medium screw bases, of both
reﬂector and PAR types, having a diameter larger than 2³/₄ in.
