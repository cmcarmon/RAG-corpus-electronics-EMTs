3 February – India – Nagpur level crossing disaster, Nagpur, Maharashtra. 55 wedding guests die when a crowded trailer being towed by a tractor was hit by a train.
27 April – Sri Lanka – Polgahawela level crossing accident, Polgahawela. Over 50 people were killed when a bus is hit by train.
7 June – Nepal – Badarmude bus explosion, Badarmude. 38 people were killed when a bus struck a land mine.
