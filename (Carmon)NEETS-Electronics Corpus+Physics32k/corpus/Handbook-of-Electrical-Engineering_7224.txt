Wireless local-area networks  (LANs)  represent  a  new  form  of  communications  among  personal  computers
inside buildings. To better understand its applicability, this paper deﬁnes the customer challenges in networking
personal  computers  as  well  as  speciﬁc  product  requirements  for  a  wireless  LAN.  These  insights  were  gained
through market studies of over 1000 corporate and government entities surveyed through different marketing
research techniques.
