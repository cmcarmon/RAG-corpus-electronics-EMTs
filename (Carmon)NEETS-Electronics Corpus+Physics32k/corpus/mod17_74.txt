The first indicator, 2, is numeric and refers to unit 2. The next indicator, A2, is alphanumeric and 
refers to assembly A2. The next indicator, A3, is also alphanumeric and refers to subassembly A3. The 
last indicator, C1, like the two previous, is alphanumeric and refers to the part C1. We have just located 
capacitor C1 on subassembly A3, which is on assembly A2, which is in unit 2 of the equipment.
