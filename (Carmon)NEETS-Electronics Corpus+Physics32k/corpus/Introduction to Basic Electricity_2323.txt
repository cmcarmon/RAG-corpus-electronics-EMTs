After	completing	this	chapter,	the	student	will		
be	able	to:
Explain	the	principles	of	capacitance.
Identify	the	basic	units	of	capacitance.
Identify	different	types	of	capacitors.
Determine	total	capacitance	in	series	and	
parallel	circuits.
Explain	RC	time	constants	and	how	they	relate	
to	capacitance.
