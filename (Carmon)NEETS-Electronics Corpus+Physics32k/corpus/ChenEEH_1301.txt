Extended Industry Standard Architecture Bus
Because MCA was proprietary, the extended industry standard
architecture (EISA) was formed to compete with it. EISA
gained much more acceptance; MCA has all but disappeared.
EISA is an improved version of ISA. It is great for high-
bandwidth bus mastering cards, such as SCSI host adapters,
but its high cost limits its usefulness for other types of cards.
