Research of Galina Tsukanova was devoted to design of Space Optical Devices. She is an author of more than 80 scientific papers, and more that 17 patents.
Some of her works are:
G. I. Tsukanova, A. V. Bakholdin, Special branches of applied optics—Study Guide. G. I. Tsukanova, G. V. Karpova, O. V. Bagdasarova, Applied optics—Study Guide, 2013. A. V. Bakholdin, G. I. Romanova, G. I. Tsukanova, Theory and Methods of Optical Devices Design—2011.
