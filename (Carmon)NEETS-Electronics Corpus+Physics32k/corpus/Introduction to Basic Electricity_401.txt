Procrastination is the act of replacing high- 
priority tasks with low-priority tasks, and thereby 
 putting off important tasks to a later time. The leading 
cause of poor productivity is procrastination. People 
tend to procrastinate for many reasons. Some of the 
reasons for procrastination are listed here:
■● The task is unpleasant.
■● The task is overwhelming.
■● The task may lead to negative consequences.
