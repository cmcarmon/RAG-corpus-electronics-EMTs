BCC is the comprehensive process in which one passes through the stages:
Unaware > Aware > Concerned > Knowledgeable > Motivated to change > Practicing trial behavior change > Sustained behavior change
It involves the following steps:
State program goals
Involve stakeholders
Identify target populations
Conduct formative BCC assessments
Segment target populations
Define behavior change objectives
Define BCC strategy & monitoring and evaluation plan
Develop communication products
Pretest
Implement and monitor
Evaluate
Analyze feedback and revise
