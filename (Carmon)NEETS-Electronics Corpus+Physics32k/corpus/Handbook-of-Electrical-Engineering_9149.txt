Hand-held systems offer the ﬂexibility of mobile systems. Integrated with telecommunication devices and
hand-held computer technology, these systems can present touchscreen interfaces and provide facilities such
as gaming, fax, e-mail, telephone, e-purse, loyalty, healthcare or driver’s license programs, security, authenti-
cation, identiﬁcation, electronic payment, secure access to networks, secure data transfer over those networks,
etc. GSM is gaining more popularity by using the cell phone as a mobile reader to personalize your phone and
provide the user with facilities such as e-mail and prepaid services.
