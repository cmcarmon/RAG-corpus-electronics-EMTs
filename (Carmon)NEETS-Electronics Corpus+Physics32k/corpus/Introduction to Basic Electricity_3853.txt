Impedance­coupling­works­just­like­RC­coupling.­
The­advantage­is­that­the­inductor­has­a­very­low­DC­
resistance­across­its­windings.­The­AC­output­signal­is­
developed­across­the­inductor­just­as­across­the­load­
resistor.­However,­the­inductor­consumes­less­power­
than­the­resistor,­increasing­the­overall­efficiency­of­
the­circuit.
