Internal ballistics - The behavior of the projectile and propellant before it leaves the barrel.
Transitional ballistics - The behavior of the projectile from the time it leaves the muzzle until the pressure behind the projectile is equalized.
Terminal ballistics - The behavior of the projectile upon impact with the target.
Trajectory of a projectile - Basic external ballistics mathematic formulas.
Rifleman's rule - Procedures or "rules" for a rifleman for aiming at targets at a distance either uphill or downhill.
