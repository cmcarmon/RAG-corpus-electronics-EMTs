179 passengers and crew, including 3 children and 3 infants, were on the aircraft at the time of the crash. Of the 179 occupants, 83 were killed, 39 suffered from serious injuries, 32 had minor injuries, while 25 were uninjured. Amongst those who perished, there were 4 crew members. 81 passengers and crew died on impact immediately after the crash and 2 passengers died at a hospital.
The passengers mostly consisted of Taiwanese and Americans.
