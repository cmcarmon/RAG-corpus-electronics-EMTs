There are many free productivity programs avail-
able for the many different types of operating systems 
on the Internet. Anything downloaded from the In-
ternet requires a word of caution regarding virus. Any 
information, program, or image downloaded from the 
Internet should be suspected of containing a virus. It

Creating a document, presentation, publication, 
or spreadsheet requires planning and then working 
through the plan. Start by making simple sketches 
on paper, showing the various pages or elements to 
be created. Include images and diagrams on how the 
pages are interconnected or flow.
