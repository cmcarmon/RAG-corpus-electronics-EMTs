The phrase ﬁeld test is used to deﬁne testing by early customers of a software product, often under a special
agreement with the software vendor. Field testing often uses live data and actual customer usage as the vehicle
for ﬁnding bugs, although prepared test cases may also occur. The defect removal efﬁciency of ﬁeld test ﬂuctuates
wildly from customer to customer and product to product, but seldom exceeds 30%.
