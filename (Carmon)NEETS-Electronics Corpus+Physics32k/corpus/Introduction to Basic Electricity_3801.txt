In­the­common-base­circuit­(Figure­37-1),­the­­input­
signal­enters­the­emitter-base­circuit,­and­the­output­
leaves­from­the­collector-base­circuit.­The­base­is­the­
element­common­to­both­the­input­and­output­circuits.
In­the­common-emitter­circuit­(Figure­37-2),­the­
input­signal­enters­the­base-emitter­circuit,­and­the­
output­signal­leaves­from­the­collector-emitter­circuit.­
The­emitter­is­common­to­both­the­input­and­output­
circuits.­This­method­of­connecting­a­transistor­is­the­
most­widely­used.
