Video Electronics Standards Association Local Bus
The video electronics standards association (VESA) local bus
(VLB). VLB is great for video cards, but its lack of a good bus
arbiter limits its usefulness for bus mastering cards, and its
moderate cost limits its usefulness for low- to mid-bandwidth
cards. Since it can coexist with EISA/ISA, a combination of all
three types of cards usually works best. Moreover, the VLB has
