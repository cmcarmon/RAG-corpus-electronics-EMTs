The information is organized into nine major sections,
which encompass the ﬁeld of electrical engineering. Each
section is divided into chapters. In all, there are 72 chapters
involving 108 authors, each of which was written by leading
experts in the ﬁeld to enlighten and refresh knowledge of
the mature engineer and educate the novice. Each section
contains introductory material,
leading to the appropriate
applications. To help the reader, each article includes two
important and useful categories: deﬁning terms and references.
Deﬁning terms are key deﬁnitions and the ﬁrst occurrence of
each term deﬁned is indicated in boldface in the text. The
references provide a list of useful books and articles for
following reading.
