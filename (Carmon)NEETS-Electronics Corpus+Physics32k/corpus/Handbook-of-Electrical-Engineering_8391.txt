As  society  has  become  more  dependent  upon  the  computer  and  computer  technology,  it  has  become
increasingly concerned with protecting the privacy of individuals and maintaining the integrity of computer
systems  against  inﬁltration—by  individuals,  groups,  and  even  on  occasion  by  governments.  Techniques  for
protecting the security of a system and ensuring individual privacy are discussed in Chapter 97.
