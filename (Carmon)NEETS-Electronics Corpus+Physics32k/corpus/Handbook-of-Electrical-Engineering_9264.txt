The phrase acceptance test is used to deﬁne testing by a speciﬁc client, as a precursor to determining whether
to  accept  a  software  product  or  not. Acceptance  testing  may  be  covered  by  an  actual  contract,  and  if  so  is  a
major business factor. The defect removal efﬁciency of acceptance testing ﬂuctuates from client to client and
product to product, but seldom exceeds 30%.
