Computer  programs  such  as  National  Instru-
ments’ Multisim, New Wave Concepts’ Circuit Wizard, 
 Cadence’s OrCAD PSpice, and Spectrum Software’s 
Micro-Cap 7 allow virtual	breadboarding, or test-
ing of a circuit and troubleshooting using virtual test 
equipment. Several of these programs shows the current 
flow in a circuit to aid in understanding how a circuit 
performs. These programs are the wave of the future.
