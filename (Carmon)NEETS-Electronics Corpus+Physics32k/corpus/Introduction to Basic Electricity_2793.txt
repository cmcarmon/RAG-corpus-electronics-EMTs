As­resistance­is­introduced­to­a­circuit,­the­phase­
angle­becomes­less­than­90°­and­the­power­curve­
shifts­to­a­more­positive­value,­showing­that­the­cir-
cuit­is­taking­more­energy­than­it­is­returning.­How-
ever,­the­capacitive­or­inductive­part­of­the­circuit­
still­stores­and­releases­energy­and­consumes­no­
power.­The­power­loss­is­due­entirely­to­the­resistance.­
­Remember,­only­the­resistive­part­of­the­circuit­con-
sumes­power.
